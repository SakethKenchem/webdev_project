Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about book availability, ordering, payments, and location.

Have a Question?

Showing all questions.

You can browse our catalogue on our Books page. Once you find the book you want, click "Send an Inquiry" or visit our Contact page to fill out the form. Select "Related to Book Purchase" as the subject, upload a screenshot or image of the book cover if you have it, and write your request in the message. We will get back to you with availability, payment, and delivery options.

Yes, we do! We can source specific books for you from local and international distributors. Please send us an inquiry via our Contact page with the title, author, and ISBN if you have it. We will search for the book, check delivery timelines, and send you a price estimate.

We accept cash, credit/debit cards (Visa/Mastercard) at our physical shop in Nairobi. For deliveries or mobile pick-ups, we support M-Pesa payments through our Buy Goods Till Number (Till: 123456). Payment details are shared once an order is confirmed.

Yes, we offer delivery across Kenya. Within Nairobi, we use local courier services (charges between KES 200 - KES 400 depending on location). Outside Nairobi, we deliver via courier services like G4S or Easy Coach at standard courier rates. Deliveries are made within 24 to 48 hours after payment verification.

We accept returns within 7 days of purchase for an exchange or refund, provided the book is in its original, undamaged condition and accompanied by the receipt. Special order books sourced specifically for a customer are not eligible for returns unless they are defective or damaged upon delivery.

Our physical bookstore is located at Chapter House Building, 2nd Floor, along Kenyatta Avenue, in Nairobi CBD, Kenya. We are open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:30 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.
